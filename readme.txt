Dependencies:
opencv-python
pytesseract
pyenchant
googletrans
gtts
pyglet

Instructions:
1. Run "laser_tracker.py"
2. A window will popup containing the video stream of your computer's camera
3. By projecting a red laser onto a a piece of paper containing English words, the program will find the word within the closest proximity of the laser pointer and translate that word into Chinese then output it through the users audio output

To end the program, press q while the computer is focused on the camera window
